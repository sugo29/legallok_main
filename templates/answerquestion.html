As a family lawyer practicing in Mumbai, I can outline the process:

  1. File a petition under Section 26 of the Hindu Marriage Act or Section 38 of the Special Marriage Act
  2. Required documents:
    • Divorce decree copy
    • Child's birth certificate
    • Your ID and address proof
    • Affidavit stating proposed custody arrangement
  3. The court will consider:
    • Child's preference (if age 12+)
    • Parent's financial stability
    • Existing caregiving arrangement

Since you have mutual agreement, the process should be straightforward. I recommend:

  1. Draft a parenting plan with visitation schedule
  2. File at family court where child resides
  3. Hearing typically takes 3-6 months
